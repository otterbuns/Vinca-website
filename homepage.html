About Us

A SHORT INTRODUCTION TO VINCA

Vinca is an independent illustrated magazine exploring what it means to survive—physically, emotionally, and creatively—in a changing world. Through original artwork, visual storytelling, and thoughtful essays, we document resilience, vulnerability, and the quiet strength found in everyday lives. Each issue is a space for reflection, where survival is not just about endurance, but about adaptation, imagination, and hope.
